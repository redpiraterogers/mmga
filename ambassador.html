🧘

Without ambassadors seeding the network through personal credibility and LinkedIn reach, cold user acquisition costs are prohibitive. 30% fairly compensates the people driving the entire growth engine.

NEW MINT — NOT TAKEN FROM RECRUIT The recruit mines 1 $MONK and receives 1 $MONK in full. The protocol mints an additional 0.3 $MONK to the ambassador. No dilution of the recruit's reward. Supply expands proportionally with real network activity — a healthy tokenomic signal.
📈
ALIGNED INCENTIVES At 30%, ambassadors earn more when recruits meditate longer and more consistently. This aligns financial incentives directly with MONKCOIN's mission: more humans meditating more often. The commission structure is the growth hack and the product simultaneously.

BENCHMARKED VS WEB3 REFERRALS Leading earn-to-X protocols offer 10–20% referral rates. MONKCOIN's 30% reflects the early-stage priority of network growth — consistent with how Axie Infinity, STEPN, and Helium incentivised their earliest community builders at above-market rates.

After Year 5, the recruit's mining continues but the ambassador commission sunsets. This protects long-term token supply integrity while still giving ambassadors a significant multi-year income window — long enough to be genuinely valuable, finite enough to be economically sustainable.
