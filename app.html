🎓 SETUP GUIDE FOR BEGINNERS
📱 STEP 1: USE THE RIGHT BROWSER
This app works on desktop/laptop computers using these browsers:

✅ Chrome (recommended) - Download Chrome
✅ Brave - Download Brave
✅ Edge - Built into Windows
✅ Firefox - Download Firefox

❌ Safari may have issues - use Chrome instead
❌ Mobile browsers not fully supported yet
💼 STEP 2: INSTALL METAMASK WALLET
MetaMask is a free digital wallet for cryptocurrency. Think of it like a digital bank account.

Installation (5 minutes):
1. Go to metamask.io
2. Click "Download" button
3. Click "Install MetaMask for Chrome" (or your browser)
4. Click "Add to Chrome"
5. Click "Add Extension"
6. The MetaMask fox icon appears in your browser toolbar (top-right corner)

Setup Your Wallet (10 minutes):
1. Click the fox icon
2. Click "Create a new wallet"
3. Create a strong password (you'll need this every time!)
4. Watch the short security video
5. Write down your 12 secret words on paper (VERY IMPORTANT!)
6. Confirm your secret words
7. Done! Your wallet is ready! 🎉
⚠️ IMPORTANT SECURITY TIPS
Your 12 secret words (seed phrase):
✅ Write them on paper and store safely
✅ Never share with anyone, ever
✅ MMGA team will NEVER ask for them
✅ These words restore your wallet if you forget password

Your password:
✅ Used to unlock MetaMask on your computer
✅ Store it somewhere safe (password manager)
✅ If you forget it, you'll need your 12 words to recover
🔌 STEP 3: CONNECT YOUR WALLET
Once MetaMask is installed:

1. Come back to this MMGA meditation app
2. Click the "Connect Wallet" button (top-right)
3. MetaMask popup will appear
4. Click "Next"
5. Click "Connect"
6. The app will ask to switch to Polygon network
7. Click "Approve" or "Switch Network"
8. You're connected! The button will show "Connected" ✅
📹 STEP 4: ALLOW CAMERA ACCESS
The app needs your camera to detect meditation:

1. When the page loads, your browser will ask: "Allow camera?"
2. Click "Allow" or "Yes"
3. Your video feed will appear on screen
4. You'll see red/green dots on your eyes

Troubleshooting:
• No camera popup? Check if your browser blocked it (look for camera icon in address bar)
• Click the camera icon and select "Allow"
• Still not working? Refresh the page and try again
🧘 STEP 5: START MEDITATING!
Once everything is set up:

1. Sit comfortably with spine straight
2. Close your eyes
3. Watch the counter increase: +1 token per second
4. Meditate for as long as you want
5. Click "MINT TO BLOCKCHAIN" to save tokens permanently
6. Click "END SESSION" when done

Your $MONK tokens will appear in MetaMask!
